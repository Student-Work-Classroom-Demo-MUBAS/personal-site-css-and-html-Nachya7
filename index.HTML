MyProjects

Drip Rate Monitor(RICE 360° Internship Project)

As part of the RICE 360° Internship Program, we developed a drip rate monitoring device to allow health workers to be able to track IV fluid rates accurately. We worked with 4D Workshop4 IDE to design the screen layout and figma to protoype the user interface and flow. The Technical Assistance team handled the circuit design and Electronics. We successfully presentated the project and others in lilongwe where a conference was held.

Neonatal Suctioning Training Model(RICE 360° Internship Project)

On this project we focused on creating a training device to help healthcare wrkers practice suctioning for neonates with breathing difficulties caused by amniotic fluid blockage.

Smart Home System

A class group project where we implemented a smart home automation system that used various sensors and actuators to monitor and control the home environment. It had features like temperature, smoke detection, light automation and intrusion detetion.

First Global Challenge(Team Malawi)

I participated in the First Global Challenge 2021, representing Team Malawi in a global robotics and innovation competition. The event focused on addressing real-world challenges through STEM, and our team contributed by designing and programming a robot that could help in hospitals by transporting food to patients who have covid so that health professionals don't have a risk of getting sick as well. The years theme was Discover, Recover, Rebuild and this highlighted the role of STEM inn COVID-19 recovery. It was a colllaborative and eye-opening experience that sharpened my problem-solving and teamwork skills.
